Background

Local businesses are a major factor that drives today’s national economy. In the United states, these businesses create more than two-thirds of the total new jobs and according to a report published by the Office of Advocacy for the U.S. small business administration, they account for 44% of the nation’s economic activity. However unlike large companies and corporations, these local businesses are more prone to the effects of events such as COVID where the customer base can decrease significantly. A solution that aims to bring customers to shop at these businesses can stimulate the local economies and reinvigorate the job market that has been affected.

What is CloudBerry?

CloudBerry is a platform that aims to support local businesses, especially during these times with COVID-19. We provide a space where local business owner can catalog their items, and customers can go out and search for product from these bsuiness to buy.

How it works

Cloudberry is powered by Google's Vision AI platform. Using various computer vision techniques from optical text recognition, image labelling, object identification, and image product search, CloudBerry allows business owners to seamlessly catalog product with little hastle and customers to search for the products using pictures. On the business side, Cloudberry uses cloud vision to automatically label and categorize products from the images that business owners upload. This aims to cut down on cataloging time and also provides a general standard when comparing products from various different businesses to each other. On the customer side, CloudBerry uses google cloud's trained machine learning algorithms to be able to search for similar products with just a picture.
